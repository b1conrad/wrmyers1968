W. R. Myers class of 1968

About wrmyers68.com

This web site is for the 1968 graduating class of W. R. Myers High School located in Taber, Alberta, Canada. Information contained herein is on the public Internet, and is intended for the use of the graduates, their friends and families, and other interested parties. Content is copyright of the respective contributors.

A brief excerpt from our yearbook, DAWN '68, starts the page of each member of the graduating class. Each such page has a short URL that can be easily shared. For example, the page for Linda Fong, the yearbook editor, is at http://wrmyers68.com/292 and the pages for other graduates are linked from the site's index page.

If you are a graduate, and wish to gain control of your own page, contact Bruce Conrad by private message on facebook or linkedin, or by email to wrmyers68 at sanbachs dot com asking for your private link. While he owns and operates this site on your behalf, you own and can control your own page. With the private link, you can also make comments or report items of interest on the pages of your fellow graduates.
